Ask us anything →
Are there any restrictions for operating hours?
+
CoKarma is a flexible coworking space. We are accessible round the clock — 24/7, 365 days a year — for our monthly members. For temporary bookings such as day passes and meeting rooms, our spaces are available from 8am to 8pm.
Are different types of workspaces available?
+
We offer pre-built "Private Cabins" for 2 to 50 people and customised "Managed Offices" from 20 to 2,000 people. We also have flexible options for individuals and freelancers — "Dedicated Desks" and on-demand "Day Passes". For quick meetings, we have a range of "Meeting Rooms" and conference spaces.
Can I customise the office space to fit my needs?
+
Yes, without a doubt! CoKarma provides personalised workspaces based on your preferences and brand personality. Choose from a variety of office styles that can be customised and created specifically for your company. We build fully managed, customised offices for enterprise clients with seat requirements from 20 to 2,000 seats.
What are the benefits of a shared flexible space?
+
Community is the biggest benefit. If you're a startup or a small-to-medium business, chances are you'll find complementary services — recruitment, accounting, digital marketing — right on your floor or within the CoKarma network. Beyond community, you get premium infrastructure, zero capital expenditure, and the flexibility to scale up or down as your team changes.
How do I book an office space?
+
You can call us at +91 99890 16041 or fill in the "Request a call back" form below. Alternatively, navigate to our locations page and book an on-demand day pass or meeting room directly from our website.
What amenities are included?
+
Every CoKarma space includes high-speed business-grade internet, 100% power backup, fully equipped conference and meeting rooms, modern design with breakout areas and game zones, and unlimited free tea and coffee. We also provide event spaces for business and team meetings, along with a vibrant community of 20,000+ members.
Why CoKarma?
+
CoKarma is one of the fastest-growing coworking spaces in Hyderabad, accessible 24/7 throughout the year. Our spaces are vibrant, community-driven environments that foster creativity, networking, and productivity — providing not just a desk and a chair, but a world of opportunities, services, and connections to help your business grow.
